I am currently a postdoc at the Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science (AMSS) of CAS, working with Professor Shihua Zhang.

I graduated from School of Mathematical Sciences at Peking University in June 2017 under the supervision of Professor Jinzhu Jia. From August 2015 to August 2016, I studied in Statistics and Data Science at Carnegie Mellon University as a visiting student under the supervision of Professor Jing Lei. After graduation I worked as a data scientist at Percent Technology for a year and a half.

Research Interests:
  • High-dimensional Statistical Inference.
  • Bioinformatics & Biostatistics.
  • Statistical Machine Learning.
